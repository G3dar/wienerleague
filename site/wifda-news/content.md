## 1. WIFDA and Wiener League Select Oktoberfest DTLV as World Championship Venue

- **Category:** News
- **Date:** MAR 5, 2026
- **Byline:** Wiener League News Desk
- **Image Caption:** The World Championship field will be decided on September 27 in Las Vegas.
- **Featured:** Yes
- **Image:** images/wifda-world-championship-location-1RjwAZHG.jpg

The World International Federation of Dachshund Athletics (WIFDA) and the Wiener League have jointly announced that the 2026 WIFDA World Championships will be held at Oktoberfest DTLV in Las Vegas, Nevada on September 27, 2026.

The selection marks the first time WIFDA has sanctioned a World Championship event at the iconic Las Vegas festival, which draws tens of thousands of attendees annually.

"This is the biggest stage in dachshund racing," said a WIFDA spokesperson. "Oktoberfest DTLV provides the atmosphere, the infrastructure, and the audience that a World Championship demands."

The event will feature a full championship bracket — qualifying heats, semifinals, and a championship final — with the winner determined by the first nose across the finish line on a regulation 30-yard track.

League officials confirmed that the top competitors from the current season will receive invitations to compete based on standings and qualifying performance.

The partnership between WIFDA and the Wiener League ensures that all races will be conducted under official WIFDA regulations, with credentialed judges and standardized competition protocols.

Tickets and competitor registration details are expected to be announced in the coming weeks.

For more information about WIFDA, visit the federation's official site.
